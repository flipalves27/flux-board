## Board copilot

On a board, the **Copilot** (when the plan includes it) uses your board context. It is an operational assist surface.

## Help Fluxy in this manual

**Manual Fluxy** only reads the help text — not your board state. It explains the product, not "what is blocked for you this week."

## Suggested use

- Copilot for *doing work*.
- This manual (and its Fluxy) for *how the app is meant to work*.
