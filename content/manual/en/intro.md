This guide explains the **Flux-Board platform** — boards, sprints, reports, billing, and most of what you see in the app menu. It is different from **Flux Docs (workspace)**: those live documents belong to your organization, with AI and card linking.

Use the **tree** on the left, **search** and filters on top, and the **Fluxy** panel on the right. Fluxy answers from this manual only, citing sections.

## Access

You need an active session, consistent with the rest of the app.
