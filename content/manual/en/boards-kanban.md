A **board** is the daily work surface: columns, cards, flow, and, depending on methodology, **Sprints** and related rituals. Wider portfolio governance is elsewhere in the app.

## Columns, WIP, flow

In Kanban, WIP, blockers, and flow health matter. In Scrum, use the sprint panel, increment review, and program increment links where enabled.

## Board copilot

The in-board **Copilot** (when your plan includes it) helps with operational work. This manual stays the stable reference; the copilot helps with the task at hand.

## Methodology

The methodology (Scrum, Kanban, LSS) changes menu affordances, but the core of columns and cards stays consistent.
